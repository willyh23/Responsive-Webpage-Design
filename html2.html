Tools and Techniques

GIS integrates various data sources—demographic, environmental, clinical—to build comprehensive disease maps. Common techniques include:

  • Heatmaps to show case density
  • Kernel density estimation to identify clusters
  • Temporal animation to visualize spread over time
  • Spatial joins to correlate disease with socioeconomic factors

Real-Time Dashboards

Platforms like HealthMap use GIS to provide live updates on disease outbreaks.
